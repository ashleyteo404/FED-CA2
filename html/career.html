  • 2020
    The Beginning

    This was the year that I first started trying to pick up a bit of HTML. I tried using this free app called Mimo, as per my friend's recommendation since he had a coding background. It was a rather on-off commitment so I didn't get very far since it was my 'O' Level year. Not to mention, it was pretty simple and didn't really test manage to test my understanding of concepts.

  • 2021
    Tech Council

    After my 'O' Levels, I enrolled into Anglo-Chinese Junior College (ACJC). A certain coding CCA, Tech Council, caught my eye. At first, I was apprehensive, however, I still wanted to give it a shot. The seniors and teacher were friendly, and I felt that this was a good opportunity for me to actually learn something. After engaging in C++ and learning about the fundamentals of computational thinking, it turned out that my interests was inclined towards coding and I looked forward to the next session everyday. At the end of the year, I decided to make the switch from JC to Polytechnic.

  • 2022
    Now

    It became undeniable that programming was definitely something I wanted to pursue. I don't have a niche yet so I decided to turn to a more general I.T. course. When the admissions exercise came again, I applied for Singapore Polytechnic's Diploma in Information Technology - S69. Now, I am doing my best to learn as much as I can, especially when it comes to Software Development, my desired specialisation for this course.
